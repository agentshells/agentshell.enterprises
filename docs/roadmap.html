Strategic Direction

Agent Shell's strategy centers on building long-term, reliable infrastructure for agentic development tools. Our focus is on:

  • EU Infrastructure: Maintaining all operations within European Union data centers
  • Reliability: Building systems designed for 24/7 availability and persistent operations
  • Agent-First: Infrastructure optimized for autonomous agents and long-running processes
  • Sustainability: Long-term infrastructure investments rather than short-term features

Future milestones and strategic developments will be communicated through our investor updates.
