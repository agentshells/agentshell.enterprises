About Agent Shell Enterprises

A Finnish holding company building and owning EU-hosted agentic software startups.

Mission

Agent Shell Enterprises Ltd is a holding company formed to launch and own multiple operating businesses in the agentic software space. We build companies with strong technical foundations, clear governance, and a long-term focus on execution.

Operating Subsidiaries

Our current plan is to launch two daughter companies under the Agent Shell Enterprises holding company:

  • AgentHub (planned subsidiary): The software company behind the AgentHub agentic development platform.
  • AgentShells (planned subsidiary): The hosting company providing infrastructure services for humans and agent developers.

This structure enables different operating teams with different ownership allocations based on effort and risk, and it improves risk containment and operational clarity across the group.

Company Values

Reliability

We build systems that work consistently. Predictable operations and engineering choices designed for long-term reliability.

Transparency

Clear communication, honest operations, and straightforward corporate governance. Public records and transparent business practices.
